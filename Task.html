TEA

Ingredients:

  • Tea leaves powder(chakra gold preferable)
  • sugar
  • water
  • Milk
  • cardamom
  • Ginger

Instructions

  1. First take a bowl and keep it on stove
  2. Pour some water and tea leaves powder
  3. Add sugar ginger paste and powdered cardamom
  4. Boil it until it smells like heaven
  5. Add some milk to that bowl and heat it again
  6. Take a cup and pour it into cup to feel that high
